Privacy Policy - TipCalc

Effective Date: September 2025

TipCalc (“we”, “our”, or “the App”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights as a user.

1. Information We Collect

TipCalc is designed to work without collecting any personal information.

  • No personal data collected: We do not ask for your name, email, phone number, or location.
  • Local calculations only: All tip and bill calculations are done entirely on your device.
  • Optional data: If you enable analytics or crash reporting, anonymous information such as app version or device type may be collected to improve performance.
  • Ads: If we display ads, the ad network may collect non-personal information. We do not control this data.

2. How We Use Information

We use information only to:

  • Improve app functionality and fix errors
  • Provide a better user experience

No personal data is sold, shared, or used for marketing.

3. Third-Party Services

The app may use third-party services for analytics or ads. These services may collect information as described in their own privacy policies.

4. Data Security

All calculations are processed locally on your device. Any optional analytics or crash reporting data is anonymized and secure.

5. User Rights

Since we do not store personal information, there is no personal data to delete. You may disable analytics or ads through your device settings.

6. Children’s Privacy

This app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
